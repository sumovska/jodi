Jammed Door and Bouncy Floor Repair Services Sydney

Jammed doors, bouncy floors and windows that don't open and close properly can start life as an inconvenience to you and your family. But did you know that they also may be an indication that your home's foundations have been seriously damaged?

Foundation damage can be caused by a variety of reasons, but are often the result of underground leaks or inefficient property drainage. When the earth surrounding the building's foundations becomes too wet or too dry, upheaval occurs; damaging the foundation piers and causing a number of structural problems.

If your foundations may have been compromised, make your first call to A1 Underpinning. With almost two decades of experience working with homeowners and business owners across Sydney to fix everything from jamming doors to serious cracked walls by addressing the root cause at the foundations, no other company in Sydney knows foundation repair quite like us.

Don't let small problems like jammed doors and bouncing floors spiral into much more serious problems. An evaluation from A1 Underpinning can quickly give you the peace of mind that your home's value is not being undermined by compromised foundations.
