Foundation Repair & House Underpinning Solutions in Sydney

If your home has developed jamming doors, bouncy floors and cracked or broken walls and drains, your home's foundations may be damaged, unstable or not strong enough to support your home. While problems may start as an inconvenience, unstable foundations can quickly result in expensive structural damage to your home.

The answer to unstable, damaged or compromised foundations is through a total foundation repair and home underpinning solution from A1 Underpinning - Expert house underpinning and foundation repair specialist in Sydney.

Our professional team combines decades of industry experience and the latest equipment and approaches to efficiently and effectively strengthen your home's foundations through a variety of techniques including:

  • Mass concrete underpinning, deepening and strengthening your foundations

  • Beam and base underpinning, spreading the load of your building across your existing foundations

  • Mini-pile underpinning

  • Soil strengthening.

Unlike some of our competitors, we specialise in creating customised house underpinning solutions to suit your home's unique situation. What this means for you is a guaranteed result for a price much lower than you first may have thought.

We have been working with homeowners, builders, owner-builders, engineers, businesses and insurance companies for over 18 years to deliver an unmatched residential and commercial underpinning solution to our clients in Sydney.
