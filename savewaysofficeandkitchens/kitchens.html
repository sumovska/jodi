Custom Kitchen Cabinets & Renovations
Serving Brisbane, the Gold Coast & Sunshine Coast

For too long, homeowners have only had two choices for their kitchen renovation: inferior quality kitset type kitchens or having to deal with multiple design, manufacturing, installation, plumbing companies and other tradespeople to get the job done, leading to both a high cost and a high stress level.

Thankfully, Saveways Office and Kitchens offer their clients in Brisbane, the Gold Coast and the Sunshine Coast a wide range of complete kitchen cabinet and renovation solutions that represent quality, style and compelling value for money.

When you choose to work with us, we manage your entire project, including:

  • Initial  design consultation with an experienced kitchen designer
  • Kitchen plans, computer modelling and blueprints
  • Inhouse manufacturing
  • Installation
  • Help and advice choosing tiles, benchtops, splashbacks and appliances

To start planning your dream kitchen, come and visit us at our Brisbane showroom and browse our inspirational range of kitchens. Alternatively, if you can’t make it to us, we’ll come to you for an obligation free measure and quote.

Are you a builder, designer or architect? Talk to us about our trade discounts on all our kitchen cabinetry solutions.

Contact us, visit us at our showroom or send us an online enquiry.
