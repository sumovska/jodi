Jubilee Springs are the leading torsion spring suppliers in Australia

Jubilee Springs are one of the leading specialist suppliers of torsion springs in Australia and we take great pride in our work.

Torsion springs are used in many commercial and domestic applications, where instead of offering resistance to a lineal directional force being applied along the helix, they offer resistance to a rotationally applied force.

So in other words, torsion springs are designed to be twisted or bent, whereas compression springs are designed to be compressed. Common examples of appliances which use torsion springs include:

  • Mousetraps
  • Swing down tailgates
  • Garage doors
  • CD players
  • Digital cameras
  • Clip boards
  • Clothes pegs
  • Ratchets

Torsion springs are used in any type application where a hinge or a join is subjected to a rotational force.


What are torsion springs?

Torsion springs are a helical or a spiral coil, which tends to be more closely wound than a compression spring and where the ends of the coil are attached to other components. Resistance is applied in the direction of the wind (either left or right) and the ends are sheered and protruding, so they can be attached to other components.

Torsion spring design, Australia

At Jubilee Springs we have a large range of stock torsion springs for immediate delivery. As the leading torsion spring designers in Australia, we also manufacture and supply custom-made torsion springs both nationally and internationally.
We can design any size torsion spring to your exact specifications, with either a left or right hand spiral, made from a variety of different materials and ends.

  • Leg ends are usually straight, but can be customised as required (bent, twisted, hooked or looped).
  • Leg angles can be customised to suit your needs.
  • Leg lengths are usually equal, but can be customised if required.
  • Direction of wind can be left-hand or right- Hand Helix
  • A variety of decorative or protective finishes are available.
  • Single or double body torsion springs are also available.
