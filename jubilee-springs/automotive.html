Automotive

Jubilee Springs supplies components used in the Automotive industry.

  • Engines - springs designed and manufactured to suit special requirements.
  • Doors - hinge Support springs and latches.
  • Safety Clips - wheel clips and pins etc.
  • Brakes - spring replacements, flat clips and tension springs.
  • Suspension - design and manufacture existing or specialised springs to suit your situation.

Please contact us today so as determine how we can best meet your spring and wire component needs.

We manufacture and supply custom made and stock springs.
