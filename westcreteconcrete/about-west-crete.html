All Your Concreting Needs In One

West-Crete Concrete is a family-owned and operated concreting business based in Kings Langley in Western Sydney. Established in 1985, we have created a reputation as a 100% reliable and totally professional concreting company…and that’s a reputation etched in concrete! Run by hands-on director Bob Harris, a fully licensed concrete contractor (Licence No: 151070C), West-Crete can tackle any concreting task, no matter how large or complicated. We do all types of domestic and commercial concreting, including a host of work for many local councils, such as council crossings. We specialise in decorative concrete, namely stencil concrete, coloured concrete, stamped concrete and pattern concrete, and we’ll be more than happy to help with all your concreting needs, be it a concrete slab, concrete driveway, or whatever.
